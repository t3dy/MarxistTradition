Michael Heinrich is a leading figure in the German "neue Marx-Lekture" (new Marx reading), which reinterprets Marx's value theory as fundamentally a monetary theory in which value has no pre-monetary existence independent of the exchange process. Drawing extensively on the Marx-Engels-Gesamtausgabe (MEGA) critical edition, he has challenged traditional Marxist readings on the tendency of the rate of profit to fall, the role of abstract labor, and the status of Marx's unfinished manuscripts. His introductory text on the three volumes of *Capital* has become a standard reference in value-form theoretical approaches.

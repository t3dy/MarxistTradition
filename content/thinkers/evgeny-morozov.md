Evgeny Morozov is a Belarusian-born technology critic whose work dismantles the ideological frameworks through which Silicon Valley presents its products as solutions to social and political problems. The Net Delusion challenged the 'cyber-utopianism' that assumed the internet would automatically promote democracy, demonstrating how authoritarian regimes effectively use digital technologies for surveillance and control. To Save Everything Click Here coined the term 'solutionism' to describe the ideology that recasts complex political and social questions as technical problems amenable to algorithmic fixes. Morozov's more recent work has moved toward a structural analysis of how digital infrastructure functions as a new site of capital accumulation and geopolitical competition.

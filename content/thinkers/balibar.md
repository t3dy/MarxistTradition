Étienne Balibar is a French philosopher who began as a student and collaborator of Louis Althusser, contributing to the landmark collective work Reading Capital. His subsequent work has ranged widely across political philosophy, addressing the intertwined structures of racism, nationalism, and class through a sustained engagement with the ambiguities of modern citizenship and universalism. He has developed a distinctive approach to what he calls equaliberty, arguing that the democratic principles of equality and freedom are inseparable and must be continually reinvented against new forms of exclusion.

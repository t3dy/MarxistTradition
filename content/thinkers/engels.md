Friedrich Engels was Marx's closest intellectual collaborator and co-author of the *Communist Manifesto*, and he played a decisive role in editing and publishing the second and third volumes of *Capital* after Marx's death. His independent works extended historical materialism into the domains of natural science, the history of the family, and systematic philosophical exposition. *Anti-Duhring* became the most widely read summary of Marxist theory in the Second International period.

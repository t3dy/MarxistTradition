Alex Williams is a British political theorist who, alongside Nick Srnicek, developed the intellectual framework of left accelerationism. Their #Accelerate Manifesto argued against the folk-political tendency of the contemporary left to privilege local, small-scale, and defensive actions, calling instead for a politics capable of operating at the scale of planetary capitalism. Inventing the Future extended this argument into a programmatic vision centred on automation, universal basic income, and the abolition of work as the basis for a post-capitalist society. Williams's work represents an attempt to reclaim the question of technological progress for the left against both neoliberal techno-utopianism and leftist technophobia.

Antonio Negri was an Italian political philosopher and a leading theorist of the autonomist Marxist movement. His collaborative work with Michael Hardt, particularly Empire, argued that a new form of decentralized global sovereignty had superseded classical imperialism, and that the revolutionary subject was no longer the industrial proletariat but the multitude of diverse productive subjects. His earlier Marx Beyond Marx offered an influential reading of the Grundrisse that emphasized living labor's creative autonomy as the driving force of capitalist development and its potential overcoming.

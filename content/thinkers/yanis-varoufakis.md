Yanis Varoufakis is a Greek economist and politician who served as finance minister during the 2015 confrontation between the Syriza government and the European troika. His account of that experience, Adults in the Room, provided an insider's analysis of how European institutional power operates to enforce austerity regardless of democratic mandates. His theoretical work, particularly Technofeudalism, argues that the major digital platforms have transcended capitalism itself, replacing market-based profit extraction with a new form of rent extraction — 'cloud rent' — that more closely resembles feudal relations than capitalist ones. This provocative thesis has generated significant debate within Marxist political economy about whether digital platform power represents a mutation within capitalism or a transition beyond it.

Terry Eagleton is a British literary theorist and cultural critic who has done more than perhaps any other figure to make Marxist ideas accessible to a broad English-speaking readership. His Literary Theory: An Introduction became one of the most widely read introductions to the field, situating various theoretical schools within their political and ideological contexts. Throughout his prolific career, he has defended the continuing relevance of Marxist critique against postmodernist and liberal challenges, combining theoretical rigor with polemical wit.

Tiziana Terranova is an Italian media theorist whose concept of 'free labour' — the simultaneous freedom and unpaid character of digital cultural production — became foundational for Marxist analyses of the internet economy. Her Network Culture argued that the digital economy depends structurally on the voluntary, unwaged productive activity of users who build websites, moderate forums, create content, and generate the data that constitutes the internet's real value. Terranova's work, drawing on autonomist Marxism and complexity theory, demonstrated that what appears as participatory culture is simultaneously a form of exploitation in which capital extracts value from activity that falls outside the wage relation.

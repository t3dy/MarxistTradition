Reading Capital introduced the method of symptomatic reading to Marxist theory, arguing that Marx's mature work operates with a concept of structural causality irreducible to Hegelian expressivism or empiricist models of knowledge. Althusser and his collaborators contend that Capital enacts an epistemological break from ideology to science, producing new concepts of the mode of production, overdetermination, and the social whole as a structure in dominance. The complete edition restores the contributions of Ranciere, Macherey, and Establet that were omitted from earlier translations.

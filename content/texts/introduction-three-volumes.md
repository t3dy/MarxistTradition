Heinrich's introduction presents Capital from the perspective of the neue Marx-Lekture (new Marx reading), emphasizing the monetary dimension of value theory and rejecting substantialist interpretations of abstract labor. The book covers all three volumes of Capital in a compact format, distinguishing Marx's critique of political economy from both classical and worldview Marxism. It has become a key reference point for value-form theoretical approaches to Marx across the Anglophone world.

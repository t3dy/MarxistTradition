Abstract labor is labor considered apart from its specific useful character, reduced to the common quality of being an expenditure of human labor-power in general. It is the substance of value and the basis on which qualitatively different commodities become quantitatively commensurable. Marx identifies abstract labor as one of his key critical discoveries, distinguishing it from the concrete, useful labor that produces use-values.

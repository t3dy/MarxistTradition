---
term: Forces of Production
german: "Produktivkr\u00E4fte"
source: "The German Ideology"
related: [relations-of-production, mode-of-production]
tags: [political economy, technology]
---

The forces of production comprise the means of production (tools, machinery, raw materials, land) together with the labor-power and technical knowledge employed in the production process. Their development is a central dynamic in Marx's theory of history, as advances in productive capacity generate contradictions with existing social relations. The concept underscores that production is never a purely technical affair but always shaped by and in tension with the social form in which it is organized.

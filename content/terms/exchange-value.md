---
term: Exchange-Value
german: Tauschwert
source: "Capital Vol. 1, Ch. 1"
related: [commodity, use-value, value-form]
tags: [value theory]
---

Exchange-value is the quantitative proportion in which use-values of one kind are exchanged for use-values of another kind. It appears initially as a relation between commodities, but Marx argues it is the necessary form of appearance of value, the socially necessary abstract labor objectified in the commodity. Exchange-value is thus not an intrinsic property of any single object but a social relation that expresses itself through the act of exchange.

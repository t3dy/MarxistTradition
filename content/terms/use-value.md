---
term: Use-Value
german: Gebrauchswert
source: "Capital Vol. 1, Ch. 1"
related: [commodity, exchange-value]
tags: [value theory]
---

Use-value refers to the qualitative utility of a commodity, its capacity to satisfy a particular human want or need. It is conditioned by the physical properties of the object and is realized only in consumption. While use-value is a necessary presupposition of exchange-value, it does not by itself determine a commodity's value in the Marxian sense; it is the material bearer of exchange-value.

About This Project

This companion site provides a curated introduction to the Marxist intellectual tradition. It maps the key thinkers, texts, and concepts that animate ongoing debates about Marx's Capital and the broader tradition of critical social theory. Entries are organized by interpretive tradition — value-form theory, historicist readings, structuralist Marxism, and analytical approaches — so you can see how the same texts are read differently by different schools.
